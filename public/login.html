Disclaimer & Agreement on Usage

This application provides compliance analytics driven exclusively by verified open public data published under the Brazilian Freedom of Information Act (Lei nº 12.527/2011).

Data & Regulatory Sources

  • ANP SISO-Incidentes Dataset: Analyzed dataset containing 30,054 authentic incidents reported to the upstream authority.
  • Regulatory Base: ANP Res. nº 46/2016 (SGIP), nº 43/2007 (SGSO), nº 41/2015.
  • Bureau Veritas Classification: NR 445 (Classification of Offshore Units), NR 459, NR 493.
  • Brazilian Labour Ministry: NR-37, NR-33, NR-35; Maritime Authority (NORMAM-01/DPC).

⚠️ Crucial Notice on Accountability

Accountability rests on usage: The critical link between specific service subcontractors (like Halliburton or Tejas) and these documented failures is inferred via exposure, not directly named as causation.

The ANP database registers incidents exclusively under the licensed operator. What the data strictly verifies is an exponential rise (+39,000%) in Conjunto Solidário de Barreira (CSB) failures operating on the same barrier systems maintained and deployed within the scope of these entities.

By proceeding, you agree that you understand the origin of the data, the fact-checking limitations regarding causation, and assume full accountability for how these insights and regulatory correlations are utilized or communicated.
