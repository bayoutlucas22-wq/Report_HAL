CSB Compliance Incidence Case Study
For Halliburton

Independent evidentiary analysis of comprehensive incident data from the Brazilian Agência Nacional do Petróleo (ANP) spanning 2013 to March 26, 2026. This study infers HAL-related operational safety incidents mapped strictly against Bureau Veritas offshore classification standards.

Disclaimer & Agreement on Usage

This application provides compliance analytics driven exclusively by verified open public data published under the Brazilian Freedom of Information Act (Lei nº 12.527/2011).

Data & Regulatory Sources

  • Data Cut-off Limit: March 26, 2026. All statistics, incidents, and contracts analyzed are strictly bounded to this cut-off timestamp.
  • ANP SISO-Incidentes Dataset: Analyzed dataset containing 30,054 authentic incidents reported to the upstream authority up to the cut-off date.
  • Regulatory Base: ANP Res. nº 46/2016 (SGIP), nº 43/2007 (SGSO), nº 41/2015.
  • Bureau Veritas Classification: NR 445 (Classification of Offshore Units), NR 459, NR 493.
  • Brazilian Labour Ministry: NR-37, NR-33, NR-35; Maritime Authority (NORMAM-01/DPC).
