Compliance Incidence Case Study
For Halliburton

Independent evidentiary analysis of comprehensive incident data from multiple regulatory agencies for Brazil, Argentina, Mexico, and Norway, spanning 2013 to 2025. Information is also provided for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, though this data is derived from Aramco reporting and is not a like comparison to open regulatory data. This study infers HAL-related operational safety incidents mapped against open and public data for the relevant country regulators.
